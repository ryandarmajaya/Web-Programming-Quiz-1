TOURIST

Tabanan is home to some of Bali's most iconic destinations. Tanah Lot Temple, standing on a rocky islet by the sea, is famous for its breathtaking sunsets. Jatiluwih Rice Terraces, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, showcase the island's stunning natural beauty and traditional farming culture. Meanwhile, Ulun Danu Beratan Temple, located by the serene Beratan Lake, offers a peaceful and spiritual atmosphere. Beyond these highlights, Tabanan still has plenty of hidden gems waiting to be discovered.
