FOOD

One of the delights of Tabanan is its unique traditional food. Entil is a traditional rice cake wrapped in banana leaves, usually served with side dishes, making it a comforting and authentic Balinese meal. Kuwir Nyat-Nyat is a savory duck dish cooked with rich Balinese spices, creating a deep and aromatic taste. Laklak Biu is a sweet rice cake topped with grated coconut and palm sugar, often served with banana for extra flavor. And of course, there are still many more local foods waiting to be explored
